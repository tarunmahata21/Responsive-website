What is Coding? Coding is the primary method for allowing intercommunication between humans and machines. In the nascent stages of coding, this was done using punch cards and physically placing them on the computer. Then, came computer programming languages that helped humans communicate with computers using a set of instructions. Essentially, this ‘set of instructions’ that tells the computer what it has to do is a ‘code’.

You can order your favorite meal via a mobile app, transfer money from the comfort of your home, and switch your lights on and off from your phone—even when you’re not home. At the core of all this technological advancement is coding. Teams of developers, designers, coders, and programmers work together to build this software that makes our lives so convenient. Coding has therefore become a highly valued skill, as companies the world over work towards building solutions that make their customers’ lives easier.
